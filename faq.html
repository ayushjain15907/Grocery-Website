General Questions

  • What is your return policy?

    We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Please bring your receipt and the item in its original packaging for a refund or exchange.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards.

Delivery Questions

  • How much is delivery?

    Delivery is free for orders over Rs.300. For orders under Rs.300, there is a 30 Rs. delivery fee.

  • When will my order arrive?

    Orders are typically delivered within 2-3 business days.
